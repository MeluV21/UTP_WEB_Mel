About Me

Hi, I’m Min Yoongi, known by stage names SUGA and Agust D. I'm a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and producer.

I started my journey in Daegu and eventually joined BTS under BigHit Entertainment in 2013. As Agust D, I’ve released solo mixtapes and collaborated with various artists.
